Privacy Policy for Net Worth Max

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Net Worth ("the App") is a personal finance tracking application developed
by Eugene Evangelista. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles
your information.

1. Information We Collect
   The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information,
   account information, or financial data to any server operated by the
   developer. All data you enter — including transactions, asset values,
   liabilities, watchlist items, and price alerts — is stored locally on
   your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

2. Third-Party Services
   The App fetches publicly available market data (stock quotes, indices,
   ETF prices, historical prices, and foreign exchange rates) from Yahoo
   Finance's public chart API. These requests contain only the ticker
   symbol being queried and standard HTTP headers. No personally
   identifiable information is transmitted.

3. Data Storage and Sharing
   - Your data never leaves your device.
   - The App has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and
     no tracking technologies.
   - The developer does not have access to your data.

4. Children's Privacy
   The App is rated 4+ and does not collect any data from anyone,
   including children.

5. Disclaimer
   The App is provided for informational and personal tracking purposes
   only. It does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice.
   All buy, sell, and investment decisions are made solely by the user,
   who bears full responsibility for those decisions. Market data and
   technical indicators are provided as-is without warranty. Past
   performance is not indicative of future results.
